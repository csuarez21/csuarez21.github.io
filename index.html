About

Hello! My name is Colin Suarez. I am a recent graduate from Saint Mary’s College of California where I received my M.S. in Business Analytics degree (Dec 2022). I currently work as an office manager at a chiropractic office but I am actively looking to make the career change into becoming a full-time data analyst because I realized that I’ve always enjoyed looking at data and have had an interest of telling stories through numbers.

I decided to make this career path change because I was reflecting on my undergraduate studies when I was at the University of San Francisco where I received my B.A. in Sociology with an emphasis in Criminology, Law, and Society. I started my undergraduate studies as a Computer Science major where I was able to begin learning Python and Java. I decided to switch career paths to try to become a lawyer which led me to switching majors. As time went on, I realized I enjoyed coding so I began my research on what programs I can get into in order to get back in the field which led me to come across St. Mary’s M.S. in Business Analytics program. During my time at St. Mary’s I felt that I was able to learn and grow as a coder and specifically a data analyst. With that being said, please feel free to check out the coursework and projects I was able to do during my time in this program. Thank you for your time, and hope to connect with you soon!
